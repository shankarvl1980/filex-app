Privacy Policy – FileX Manager (Dropbox Integration)

Effective Date: 2026-02-13

FileX Manager (“the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy describes how Dropbox data is accessed and handled when you connect your Dropbox account to the App.

1. Dropbox Account Access

  • Authentication is performed via Dropbox OAuth.
  • The App receives an access token and (if granted) a refresh token.
  • The App does not receive or store your Dropbox password.
  • Access is limited to the permissions granted during authorization.

2. How Dropbox Data Is Used

Dropbox data is accessed only to provide file management features requested by you, including:

  • Listing files and folders
  • Reading file metadata
  • Downloading files to your device
  • Uploading files from your device
  • Creating folders
  • Renaming, moving, and deleting files
  • Folder size analysis

The App does not access Dropbox content in the background without your interaction.

3. Data Storage

  • OAuth tokens may be stored locally on your device to keep you signed in.
  • Tokens are stored only on the device and are not sent to developer-controlled servers.
  • The App does not store your Dropbox files on external servers owned by the developer.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not:

  • Sell Dropbox data
  • Share Dropbox data with third parties
  • Use Dropbox data for advertising, profiling, or tracking

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Dropbox file content is not retained outside your device.
  • If you disconnect Dropbox inside the App, stored tokens are removed from the device.
  • You can revoke access anytime in your Dropbox account security settings.

6. Security

  • All communication with Dropbox uses HTTPS.
  • The App does not transmit Dropbox data to developer-controlled backend servers.

7. Your Choices

  • You can disconnect Dropbox at any time within the App.
  • You can revoke the App’s access from your Dropbox account settings.
  • Uninstalling the App removes locally stored app data from your device (subject to OS behavior).
