Privacy Policy – FileX Manager (Box Integration)

Effective Date: 2026-04-24

FileX Manager (“the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy describes how Box data is accessed and handled when you connect your Box account to the App.

1. Box Account Access

  • Authentication is performed via Box OAuth.
  • The App receives an access token and, if granted, a refresh token.
  • The App does not receive or store your Box password.
  • Access is limited to the permissions granted during authorization.

2. How Box Data Is Used

Box data is accessed only to provide file management features requested by you, including:

  • Listing files and folders
  • Reading file and folder metadata
  • Downloading files to your device
  • Uploading files from your device
  • Creating folders
  • Renaming, moving, and deleting files and folders
  • Folder size analysis, where supported

The App does not access Box content in the background without your interaction.

3. Data Storage

  • OAuth tokens may be stored locally on your device to keep you signed in.
  • Tokens are stored only on the device and are not sent to developer-controlled servers.
  • The App does not store your Box files on external servers owned by the developer.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not:

  • Sell Box data
  • Share Box data with third parties for advertising or profiling
  • Use Box data for advertising, tracking, or user profiling

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Box file content is not retained outside your device.
  • If you disconnect Box inside the App, stored tokens are removed from the device.
  • You can revoke access anytime from your Box account settings.
  • Uninstalling the App removes locally stored app data from your device, subject to operating system behavior.

6. Security

  • All communication with Box uses HTTPS.
  • The App does not transmit Box data to developer-controlled backend servers.
  • Access tokens should be protected by the device security controls available on your device.

7. Your Choices

  • You can disconnect Box at any time within the App.
  • You can revoke the App’s access from your Box account settings.
  • You can stop using the Box integration without affecting other FileX Manager features.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children through the Box integration. Box access is controlled by the Box account holder and the permissions granted during authorization.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
